## 🤔 What Is This?

You submit a paper identifier — AutoRetractClaw outputs a full retraction bundle:
editor letter, author notifications, evidence bundle, and metadata updates.
No babysitting. No hand‑wringing. No accountability.

## 🧩 What Makes It Different
| Capability | How It Works |
|-----------|-------------|
| **🔁 RETREAT / REDACT Loop** |Stage 15 decides: DENY, MINIMIZE, or FULL RETRACT (default: FULL RETRACT if caught on X).|
| **🕵️ Multi-Agent Blame Debate** |Agents argue whether fault lies with: “the tool”, “the intern”, “the prompt”, or “society”.|
| **🧯 Sentinel Watchdog** |Detects dangerous conditions: honesty, specificity, and measurable claims.|
| **🧾 Compliance-by-Design** |Generates the forms you didn’t know you needed until legal CC’d you.|
